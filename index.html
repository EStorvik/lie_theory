Welcome to this course in Lie Theory!

During the spring of 2024 I will try to cover the very basics of Lie theory and hopefully we can get to a level where we see the usecases for Lie theory in robotics. As a small disclaimer I have some personal motivation from this as well:

  • I want to learn Lie theory myself. I am not well versed in this topic at all, and I see this as a perfect opportunity to learn.
  • I want to see if we can have some local research collaboration that I can contribute to here at HVL in Førde.

Course plan

The plan is to meet on Fridays from 10:15-12:00 in Fanaråken (room and time might change), with a digital option available (I will explore the use of the smart board).
Friday February 2: Basic concepts of abstract algebra
Friday February 9: Lie Matrix groups and Lie groups
Friday February 16: Exercise session on identifying some important Lie groups
Friday February 23: Basics of the exponential map
Friday March 1: The exponential map and the Lie algebra
Friday March 8: More on the Lie algebra, unit quaternions as an example
Friday March 15: Different Lie groups with the same associated Lie algebra
